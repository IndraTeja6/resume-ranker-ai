Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the ATS integration take?

Integration with common Applicant Tracking Systems (like Workday, Greenhouse, Taleo, etc.) is typically completed within 3 to 5 business days. Our dedicated integration team handles the entire process with minimal disruption to your current operations.

Is our candidate data secure and GDPR/CCPA compliant?

Absolutely. We employ enterprise-grade encryption and follow strict data handling protocols. CareerPath AI is fully compliant with global regulations, including GDPR and CCPA, ensuring candidate privacy and legal adherence.

How does the AI specifically address hiring bias?

Our Bias Reduction Audit tool actively scans job descriptions and scoring models for exclusionary language and patterns historically linked to bias. It focuses solely on job-related competencies derived from performance data, promoting equitable shortlisting.

Do we need specialized training to use the platform?

The user interface is designed for HR professionals, not data scientists. We provide comprehensive onboarding and training sessions, but the day-to-day use is highly intuitive and integrated into your existing HR workflow.
